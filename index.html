About

I like to investigate genomes, and make inferences about how they evolved and how they operate. I am particularly interested in evolutionary conflict, and how they manifest at the genomic, transcriptomic, organismal and population levels. I have used bioinformatics, mathematical modeling, single cell sequencing technologies, population genetics and evolutionary theory to investigate complex biological questions. Next to my research career I really enjoy science communication, and have been part of several outreach activities.
